About us.

Our Brand.

Clashing the worlds of futurism, cybercore and y2k fun; our brand is a statement for those who move through digital noise with their own frequency.

Our Mission.

It is usually hard to find good fitted streetwear in Pakistan, let alone any y2k or tech-wear jackets. Which is why we aim to provide the Pakistani population with statement pieces and unique streetwear that we so rightfully deserve.

Our Story.

In our early life, we started in awe of the early internet and y2k tech aesthetics. And we wanted to fuse that nostalgia with the sleekness of the future. So a brand was born with the ideology of digital rebellion.
